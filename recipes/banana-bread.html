Banana Bread

Description

Sweet and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bananas
  • Baking soda
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Beat the eggs
  3. Mash bananas and sugar
  4. Mix in all other dry Ingredients
  5. Mix in lemon juice
  6. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 1 hour
