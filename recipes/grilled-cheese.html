Grilled-Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a delicious hot sandwich that's easy to create. You can use whatever kind of cheese you like, such as american cheese, swiss, or muenster.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter (or margarine)
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat your fry-pan to medium/low
  2. Butter one side of each peice of bread
  3. Assemble the sandwich
  4. Place the sandwich on the pan
  5. Flip when bottom piece is golden brown
  6. Toast until cheese is melted
    • tip - cover your pan if the cheese isn't melting
